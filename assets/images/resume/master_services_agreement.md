# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT (MSA)

This Master Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into on __________________ (effective date), by and between:

**_________________________ (Service Provider)**, a company incorporated under the laws of India, having its registered office at ___________________________ (hereinafter referred to as the “Service Provider”),

**AND**

**_________________________ (Client)**, a company incorporated under the laws of __________________ (country), having its registered office at ___________________________ (hereinafter referred to as the “Client”).

The Service Provider and the Client shall individually be referred to as a “Party” and collectively as the “Parties”.

---

## 1. Scope of Services
The Service Provider agrees to provide Data Science and related services, including but not limited to LLMs, Agentic AI, forecasting, and general data science services, as mutually agreed in writing (including via email, Statement of Work (SOW), or task management systems).

---

## 2. Engagement Model & Commitment
- The engagement shall operate on a retainer model.  
- The Service Provider shall commit a minimum of 25 hours and up to 40 hours per week, as mutually agreed.  
- Typical working hours shall be communicated in advance by the Service Provider; however, such hours shall not be binding or strictly enforced.  
- The Client agrees to a minimum weekly billing commitment of 25 hours, irrespective of task allocation.  
- All time within the committed hours shall be billable, including:
  - Development and implementation  
  - Meetings and discussions  
  - Planning, research, and documentation  
  - Time blocked due to Client-side dependencies  
- Non-utilization of allocated time shall not reduce payment obligations, provided the Service Provider remains available.  
- Work beyond agreed hours shall require prior approval and shall be billed additionally.  
- Work may be performed on weekends if required to meet commitments.  

---

## 3. Meetings & Communication
- Adhoc meetings shall be scheduled based on the freelancer(s) availability and convenience.  
- All meeting time shall be considered billable.  

---

## 4. Planning & Execution
- Weekly planning shall be conducted at the beginning of each week.  
- Deliverables and targets shall be defined based on capacity and in collaboration with the Freelancer(s).  
- A weekly review meeting shall be held to assess progress. In the absence of any disputes, the work shall be deemed accepted.  

**In case of any spillover:**
- The Service Provider shall provide a reasonable explanation, including any blockers.  
- Such spillover shall be treated as additional effort and shall not result in additional workload being carried forward to compensate in subsequent cycles, unless mutually agreed.  

---

## 5. Dependencies & Delays
Any delays caused due to Client-side dependencies (including access, approvals, or inputs) shall be treated as billable time.

---

## 6. Tools, Equipment & Infrastructure
- The Client shall provide the required working environment, including (where applicable) a laptop, access to systems, tools, and all necessary proprietary software and licenses.  
- If the Service Provider utilizes its own infrastructure, any associated costs (including cloud services and software) shall be billed separately.  
- In such cases, the Service Provider shall not be held liable for data security risks arising from the use of such infrastructure.  

---

## 7. Time Tracking
- Work shall be tracked using ___________________________ (Tracking Tool), along with a manual timesheet where required.  
- In cases where the Service Provider uses a personal laptop, tracking via the specified tool may be exempt, and manual timesheets shall suffice.  
- The Client shall have full visibility into logged hours, including a breakdown of tasks and justifications upon request.  

---

## 8. Deliverables
Deliverables include code, documentation, and other agreed outputs.  
The Service Provider shall provide reasonable support for integration and deployment.  

---

## 9. Payment Terms
- The engagement shall be billed at __________ INR per hour.  
- Invoices shall be raised biweekly.  
- Payment must be made within 7 days of invoice issuance.  

**Late Payment Penalty:**
- 2% per month (or part thereof) on overdue amounts  

The Service Provider reserves the right to pause or suspend services in case of delayed payments.  

---

## 10. Leave Policy
- Planned leave shall be communicated in advance.  
- Emergency leave shall be communicated at the earliest possible time.  
- In the event of a “no show” by either Party for more than three (3) consecutive days, the termination clause may be invoked.  
- Only actual hours worked shall be billed.  

---

## 11. Intellectual Property (IP)
- Upon full payment, all custom deliverables shall belong to the Client.  
- The Service Provider retains rights to:
  - Pre-existing tools and frameworks  
  - Reusable components and know-how  

---

## 12. Confidentiality
Both Parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all shared information.  
This obligation shall survive termination of the Agreement.  

---

## 13. Data Security
- The Service Provider shall follow reasonable security practices.  
- The Client is responsible for maintaining secure systems and access control.  
- The Service Provider shall not be liable for breaches originating from Client systems.  

---

## 14. Limitation of Liability
- The Service Provider shall not be liable for any indirect or consequential damages.  
- Total liability shall be limited to the fees paid under this Agreement.  

---

## 15. Termination
- Either Party may terminate this Agreement with 7–14 days’ written notice (email).  

**Immediate termination may occur in case of:**
- Non-payment  
- Material breach  

**Upon termination:**
- All completed work shall be invoiced and payable  
- Deliverables shall be handed over upon payment  
- Access shall be revoked
